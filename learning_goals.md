# Learning Goals

## What do I hope to learn from this internship?

I’m hoping to learn how cybersecurity work is handled in a real company, especially from a governance and compliance point of view.

Most of my experience so far has been through labs and personal projects, so I want to understand how security work looks when it involves real systems, real documentation, business requirements, and team communication.

I’m especially interested in learning more about ISO 27001, security policies, evidence collection, risk tracking, and how security controls are actually maintained in a startup environment.

## What skills am I most excited to develop?

I’m most excited to develop my skills in security governance, ISO 27001 compliance, documentation, risk management, and communicating security clearly.

I also want to keep improving my technical understanding around monitoring, vulnerability management, and incident response, but I think this internship is a good chance to build the governance side of my cybersecurity skills as well.

## What do I think I can contribute to the team?

I can contribute by being reliable, organised, and willing to take ownership of the tasks I’m given.

I’m comfortable working through information carefully, documenting findings clearly, and asking focused questions when something is unclear. I think I can help with tasks like reviewing documentation, tracking security actions, gathering evidence, and supporting the CISO with governance or compliance-related work.

I’m still early in my career, but I’m serious about learning and I want to be useful to the team.

## What is one area I feel less confident in that I’d like to improve?

One area I feel less confident in is real-world ISO 27001 and GRC work.

I understand the basic ideas around risks, controls, policies, and compliance, but I have not yet applied them much in a real company environment. I’d like to improve by seeing how these things are actually managed day to day, especially how evidence is collected, how gaps are tracked, and how security controls are maintained over time.

## What does success in this internship look like for me?

Success for me would mean completing the onboarding properly, contributing to real security tasks, and becoming someone the team can rely on.

By the end of the internship, I’d like to have a better understanding of ISO 27001, security governance, documentation, and how cybersecurity supports a real product and business.

I’d also like to leave with practical experience that I can speak about clearly, including what I worked on, what I learned, and how I contributed to Focus Bear’s security work.
